3.2 Tutorials

In addition to Sarah's lectures, all CS 330 students must attend a weekly tutorial, hosted by a PM. Tutorials usually cover the material from class the week of, or before. Tutorial sessions are spent working on exercises in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript as well as in-class group time to further develop projects. It is your responsibility to work through and understand the tutorial each week before hosting any of your sessions. Do not try to "wing it, lol."

Grading

PMs will take attendance and assign students grades on assignments based on a rubric sent out by Sarah. If it's obvious a student really did not try, they get a 0. If there are further questions about grading, please talk to the professor.

You are responsible for entering grades for your tutorial students directly in the Canvas gradebook.

TIP:Do not be afraid to take a few points off assignments. It does not make you mean. On the other hand, do not go off on a power trip penalizing students for small errors. Balance is key. :)
