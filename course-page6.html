3.1 Overview

Overview of Responsibilities

As PMs, your primary responsibilities are:

  • Leading weekly tutorials of 20+ students.
  • Complete grading in a timely manner.
  • Holding weekly office hours.
  • Answering questions on Piazza.

Your position is paid for 6 hours per week, so the total time spent should land around there.

Preparation

We advise:

  • Before each tutorial, you should have worked through the tutorial and understood it completely.
  • Before your mentor hours, you should have worked through the homework assignment for that week, and understood it completely.

Feel free to ask questions on Slack!
