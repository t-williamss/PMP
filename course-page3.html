2.2 Course Versions

You may have taken a different version of the course than you are currently PMing. If so, here is a brief summary of the differences between past course iterations.

With Sarah Van Wart, Winter 2020 - Present

  • No attendance quizzes.
  • Design principles are to be considered in student project.
  • Assignments strongly leaning towards implementation and critiquing the social and ethical ramifications of web design.
  • Discussion sections held weekly by PM.

With Sarah Van Wart & Eleanor O'Rourke, Winter 2019

  • Discussion sections held weekly by PM.
  • Attendance quizzes each lecture.
  • Greater emphasis on design critiques and understanding Norman's design principles

With Marcelo Worsley, Fall 2019

  • Greater emphasis on accessibility in project design.
  • Attendance quizzes each lecture.
